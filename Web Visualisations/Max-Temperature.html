Max Temperature

The Graph shows us that the cities nearest the equator (0 Latitude) are the ones that record the highest temperatures. Cities around 60 degrees north of Earth's equator record max temperatures of around 50°F which is equal to around 10°C.

These temperatures are considerably lower than those recorded on the southern hemisphere and those closest to the equator.
